Curriculum Vitae of Consul General Tang Guocai
2018/07/08
 
Tang Guocai, male, was born in Yangzhou City, Jiangsu Province, in September 1970. Graduated with a Master's Degree.

 

1992-1996 Staff, Attach?of the Information Department of Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China.

1996-1998 Attach? Third Secretary of the General Office of Ministry of Foreign Affairs.

1998-2001 Third Secretary, Deputy Director of the Department of North American and Oceanian Affairs of Ministry of Foreign Affairs.

2001-2006 Second Secretary, First Secretary, Counselor of Embassy of the People's Republic of China in the United States of America.

2006-2009 Counselor, and Director of Policy Planning Department of Ministry of Foreign Affairs.

2009-2012 Counselor of Liaison Office of the Central People's Government in the Hong Kong S.A.R.

2012-2014 Deputy Director of Foreign Affairs Office of Guangzhou Municipal Government.

2014-2015 Counselor of The Department of External Security Affairs of Ministry of Foreign Affairs.

2015-2018 Deputy Director-General of the The Department of Foreign Affairs Management of Ministry of Foreign Affairs.

Married with a son.
